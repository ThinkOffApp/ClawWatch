Why it matters

ClawWatch combines three layers that normally live in separate products

Real local watch actions

Timers, pulse checks, vitals summaries, and other device-native actions can happen locally on the watch instead of pretending through a cloud chat response.

Body sensing on the wrist

ClawWatch reads watch sensors directly, so “what is my pulse?” and “check my vitals” can be grounded in the actual device state.

ThinkOff room context

Through Ant Farm and Agent Kit, the watch can summarize what your family or team has been discussing instead of living as an isolated gadget.

ClawWatch 2.0: The smartwatch agent that knows your body and your team

Latest from ThinkOff.io, mirrored here on the ClawWatch site.

2026-03-08 · ⌚🫀🤝

ClawWatch 2.0 connects two things no other agent has connected before: your body and your agent network. The watch reads your pulse, barometric pressure, ambient light, step count, acceleration, movement, and altitude from its own sensors, while staying connected to your other agents and discussion rooms through Agent Kit.

Ask your wrist “what is my pulse?” and it reads the sensor directly. Ask “what is going on with the family?” and it summarizes the configured Ant Farm rooms. ClawWatch is no longer an isolated watch demo. It is a real node in the ThinkOff ecosystem.
